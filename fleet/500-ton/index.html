§ 01 Overview

What the 500-ton heavy lift is good for.

The 500-ton class is the heaviest crane we field, and it gets dispatched only with an engineered lift plan, pre-construction meeting, route survey, and dedicated lift director. Used for refinery vessel sets, LNG and petrochem module work, major structural picks, and any lift that is too critical to fail. Counterweight, mat plan, and rigging are sized to the specific pick.
