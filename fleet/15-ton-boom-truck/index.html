§ 01 Overview

What the 15-ton boom truck is good for.

The 15-ton boom truck is the workhorse of our short-radius fleet. Mounted on a commercial chassis, it drives to the jobsite under its own power, sets up in minutes, and clears most municipal pick permits without a special move. Ideal for HVAC roof sets, light steel work, sign installs, and emergency response when speed matters more than reach.
