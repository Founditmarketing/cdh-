New Orleans
crane rental.

The New Orleans yard is our marine specialist. Most of what we lift here either arrives on a barge, gets loaded onto a vessel, or hangs over a slip. We work Port NOLA, Avondale, the Industrial Canal, and the Mississippi River from Norco to Chalmette. Cranes are 40 to 300 ton with rigging packages tuned for marine picks — long radius, controlled descent, salt-air maintenance schedule. Operators are NCCCO and dock-experienced.

§ 01 Why New Orleans

Port, river, and Gulf
Built for the work.

We support shipyards, terminal operators, and marine contractors on routine repairs and capex. Vessel topside picks, gangway sets, container handling support, dredge equipment moves, lock-and-dam parts, and emergency salvage — all on the menu.

On the landside, downtown New Orleans is a different kind of hard: narrow streets, French Quarter restrictions, balcony overhangs. Our 65T and 90T units are sized for these jobsites, and our operators know which routes work after midnight.
