Lake Charles
crane rental.

The Lake Charles yard is built for industrial scale. Westlake, Sulphur, and the Calcasieu Ship Channel run on continuous turnarounds, expansions, and LNG capex — and we run the cranes that move the steel. The yard carries 40 to 500 ton capacity, with crawler and all-terrain mix biased toward refinery and petrochemical work. NCCCO operators are pre-badged at Phillips 66, Citgo, Sasol, Westlake Chemical, and the LNG operators on the channel.

§ 01 Why Lake Charles

Refining, petrochem, and LNG
Built for the work.

We were on the ground for Calcasieu Pass and Cameron LNG mods and have stayed there for ongoing operations and maintenance. Turnaround season means dispatch runs at sustained tempo: pre-stage cranes the week before, rotate operators on 12-hour shifts, return cranes for inspection between units.

After Hurricane Laura we ran emergency lifts across the parish for sixty consecutive days. Storm response is part of the muscle memory of this yard.
