§ 01 Why Baton Rouge

Capital region & industrial corridor
Built for the work.

The corridor between Baton Rouge and Donaldsonville drives most of our 24/7 emergency calls — pump pulls, exchanger swaps, vessel sets, and shutdown support. We are pre-qualified at the major plants and can pre-stage operators and rigging for planned outages.

On the commercial side, we work LSU, state Capitol, Mid City, and Bluebonnet projects with GCs we have known for fifteen years. Quotes come back fast, cranes show up on time, and the operators do not embarrass anyone.
