Lafayette
crane rental.

CDH Crane Rentals was founded in Lafayette in 2003 and our HQ yard still anchors every job we run across the Gulf South. The Lafayette yard carries the full fleet — from 15-ton boom trucks for HVAC sets in Oil Center high-rises to 500-ton hydraulic cranes for refinery and offshore module work. Dispatch is staffed 24/7, NCCCO-certified operators stage from this yard nightly, and we keep a senior lift planner on call for critical picks.

§ 01 Why Lafayette

headquartered in Acadiana
Built for the work.

Over two decades of Acadiana lifts mean we have rigging plans, permit knowledge, and traffic-control relationships dialed in for Lafayette Parish, St. Martin, and Vermilion. We have set chillers on every commercial roof in town, picked oilfield modules at the Port of Iberia, and supported steeple and steel sets from Carencro to Abbeville.

Lafayette is also our parts and maintenance hub. Every crane in the fleet rotates through this yard for OEM-spec service and load-chart re-verification, so the rig that shows up at your jobsite is the one we just inspected.
