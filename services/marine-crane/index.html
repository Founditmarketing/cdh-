§ 01 What it is

Marine crane services for ports, docks, and shipyards

Marine work has its own rules: salt air maintenance, long-radius picks over water, tide and wind windows, harbor pilot coordination, and rigging tuned for controlled descent. Our New Orleans yard is the marine specialist; Baytown and Lake Charles share the load on channel work. Operators are dock-experienced and NCCCO certified.

§ 02 What is included
  • Marine-spec rigging packages
  • Long-radius pick capability
  • Salt-air maintenance schedule
  • Coordination with harbor pilots and terminal ops
  • Tide and wind window planning
  • Controlled-descent rigging
§ 03 When you need it

Anything that involves a vessel, a barge, a dock, or a slip. Topside picks, gangway sets, container support, dredge equipment, lock-and-dam parts.
