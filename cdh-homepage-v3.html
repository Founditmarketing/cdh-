§ 06 Common questions

Crane rental,
straight answers.

What procurement teams, GCs, and refinery planners ask before they pick up the phone. Don't see your question? Call dispatch at 337.962.3999.

What size cranes does CDH Crane Rentals offer?

+

Our fleet runs from 15-ton boom trucks up to 500-ton hydraulic cranes, including rough-terrain, all-terrain, and crawler classes. We size each crane to the lift radius, pick weight, and jobsite footprint.

Do you provide a certified operator with the rental?

+

Yes. Every operated rental includes an NCCCO-certified operator. We also provide a signaler and rigging when the job calls for it. Bare rentals are available when you have your own certified crew.

What's the difference between an operated and a bare crane rental?

+

Operated rental includes a CDH operator, fuel, insurance, and ongoing maintenance. Bare rental delivers the crane only; the customer supplies a certified operator and assumes operational responsibility. Most refinery, marine, and commercial customers choose operated.

How far in advance should I book a crane?

+

Two to three weeks is ideal for planned lifts, especially anything 175 ton or larger. We also run 24/7 emergency dispatch for shutdowns, storm response, and same-day picks when capacity allows.

Do you offer 24/7 emergency crane service?

+

Yes. Our dispatch desk is staffed twenty-four hours for urgent lifts across Louisiana and East Texas, including refinery upsets, marine emergencies, and storm response.

Which cities and regions does CDH serve?

+

We operate from five yards: Lafayette (HQ), Baton Rouge, Lake Charles, New Orleans, and Baytown, TX. We routinely cover Houma, Morgan City, New Iberia, Sulphur, Westlake, Port Allen, Gonzales, LaPlace, Pasadena, Deer Park, and the wider Gulf South.

Are you insured and approved for refinery and petrochemical work?

+

Yes. CDH maintains current ISN, Avetta, and OSHA compliance, full general liability and equipment insurance, and a documented safety program built for refinery turnarounds and petrochemical expansions.

How do I get a quote?

+

Call dispatch at 337-962-3999, email office@cdhrentals.com, or submit the dispatch form on this page with the lift weight, radius, date, and jobsite. Most quotes come back the same business day.
