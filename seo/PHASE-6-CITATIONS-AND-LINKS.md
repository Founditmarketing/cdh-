# Phase 6 — Citations & Local Link Building

The goal is twofold: (1) saturate every directory that contributes to local trust signals with consistent NAP, and (2) earn legitimate inbound links from local and industry sources that prove CDH is a real, established Gulf South business.

### Submission cadence
- **Week 1**: Tier 1 + Tier 2 (10–15 listings)
- **Week 2–3**: Tier 3 (industry-specific)
- **Week 4–6**: Tier 4 (local chambers + associations — these often need membership applications and money)
- **Ongoing**: monthly audit of NAP consistency across all submitted directories. Tools: BrightLocal, Whitespark, Moz Local. Free alternative: manual quarterly spot check + a citation tracker spreadsheet.

## 6.2 Local link building targets

Citation-only directories give baseline local trust. The next level is earning links from real local sites with editorial weight.

### Parish & municipal sources
- Parish economic development sites (Lafayette Parish, East Baton Rouge, Calcasieu, Orleans, Harris County) — pitch a "company profile" or "vendor spotlight" submission.
- City permit / contractor lists (where applicable).

### Industry buyer directories
- Major refiners and petrochem operators maintain approved vendor lists for crane services. Apply to be on:
  - **Lake Charles**: Phillips 66 (Westlake), Citgo (Lake Charles), Sasol (Westlake), Westlake Chemical, Cameron LNG, Venture Global Calcasieu Pass.
  - **Baton Rouge corridor**: ExxonMobil (Baton Rouge), Dow (Plaquemine), Shell (Geismar / Convent), BASF (Geismar), Marathon (Garyville), Nucor (Convent).
  - **New Orleans region**: Valero (Norco / St. Charles), Phillips 66 (Belle Chasse), Cornerstone Chemical.
  - **Baytown corridor**: ExxonMobil (Baytown), Chevron Phillips (Cedar Bayou), Covestro (Baytown), LyondellBasell (Channelview), INEOS (La Porte), Equistar.
- Each owner typically requires ISN, Avetta, or owner-specific contractor management onboarding. Use existing compliance as a wedge to get on the directory.

### Local news + trade press
- Place 1 sponsored or news placement per quarter in each major-yard city's business journal. Cost-effective options:
  - Lafayette: *The Acadiana Advocate* / *Acadiana Business* / *Daily Advertiser*
  - Baton Rouge: *Greater Baton Rouge Business Report* / *The Advocate*
  - Lake Charles: *American Press*
  - New Orleans: *New Orleans CityBusiness* / *Times-Picayune*
  - Baytown / Houston: *Houston Business Journal* / *Baytown Sun*
- Trade publications worth pitching: *American Cranes & Transport*, *Crane Hot Line*, *Lift & Hoist International*, *ENR Texas & Louisiana*, *Construction Equipment Guide* (Southeast).

### Sponsorships & community
- Sponsor 1–2 industry events per year per yard city (SC&RA annual conference, local AGC events, refinery safety awards, hurricane preparedness drills). Sponsorship pages almost always link back.
- Sponsor local youth sports / community events for hyper-local link equity. Most chambers and city sites publish sponsor lists with links.

### Resource page outreach
- Several construction-management and OSHA training sites maintain "crane rental resources" or "Gulf South contractor" pages. Reach out with the [/learn/](../learn/) educational pages as link bait — they're useful enough to earn a citation without needing to ask for one.

## 6.3 What NOT to do

- Avoid private blog networks (PBNs), paid link networks, and "guest posting for $X" services. Google manual actions will follow.
- Avoid generic article directories (EzineArticles, GoArticles) — they pass zero value and look spammy on a backlink audit.
- Avoid bulk citation submissions from cheap overseas vendors — many submit incorrect NAP and trigger duplicate listing issues that take months to clean up.

## 6.4 Tracking

Maintain a single citations + links spreadsheet:
- Sheet 1: every citation submitted (directory, date submitted, status, listing URL, NAP version).
- Sheet 2: every earned inbound link (source, anchor text, target URL, date earned).
- Run a monthly Ahrefs / Semrush / Moz / free backlink check to surface new and lost links.

Targets at 6 months: **80+ citations live + consistent**, **30+ earned editorial inbound links**.
